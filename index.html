Welcome

Developed at the University of Reading School of Psychology and Clinical Language Sciences. This is not developed by the same team as JASP (although JASP is great).

This textbook is under-development, and is aimed at students in the school of Psychology and Clinical Language Sciences. The aim will be to focus on statistics taught in Reading (for now MSc year) using the following free software:

JASP (open-source)
Microsoft Excel (can be free online with an outlook account)
Python (open-source)
R (open-source)
